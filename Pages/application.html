Teacher Assistant Description

A teacher assitant is a one individual student that get's selected in order to aid the professor. One of the requirements is that the student has taken the class they'll be assisting with before and is in good academic standing. Students selected to be Teacher Assistants may get tuition discounts as well. Please speak with your advisor in order to get all of the lastest incentives for becoming a teacher assistant
